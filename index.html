This domain is shorter and easy to remember. It's more likely for a visitor to be able to remember this domain name, ultimately increasing the amount of clients able to be reached.

The full title/Business name will appear at the top of the website easily letting visiors know they have reached the desired destination. As well, full business name and city data will appear in so many other places on the site and in the meta data (the hidden data that search engines look for), that it won't affect search engine traffic in a negative manner, nor will it confuse the site visitor. Instead, it will allow them an easier name to remember after having seen public advetising. It will also decrease the amount of lost traffic due to long URL names (domain name/website address) and spelling errors.
