Target Audience:

"An estimated 6.2 million Americans age 65 and older are living with Alzheimer's dementia in 2021. Seventy-two percent are age 75 or older " accorting to Alzheimers Association.

80% of those patients are receiving care in their homes, from a family memmber. "Approximately two-thirds of dementia caregivers are women, about one in three caregivers (34%) is age 65 or older, and approximately one-quarter of dementia caregivers are “sandwich generation” caregivers, meaning that they care not only for an aging parent, but also for children under age 18."-cdc.gov

The target audience for Williamsburg Dimentia Consulting site is primarily women ages 40-65 years old, with a household income in the range of $50,000-$115,000. Has graduated high school and has some college experience, possibly a college degree. Is likely in the middle-career and also has children under the age of 18 they currently still care for. The target market for this site visitor is a person who values family, and the relationship they maintain with their loved one. They likely are beginning their journey of a dementia diagnosis or have been trying to self-manage the diagnosis and don't know where to turn next.

Scenarios:

  1. How do I help my loved one with dementia?
  2. What are the symptoms of early onset dementia?
  3. How can I get help for my parent with dementia?
  4. Which resources are available to dementia patients?
  5. WHow do you recognize dementia?
